I am a postdoctoral researcher in the department of Computing and Mathematical Sciences at Caltech, hosted by Prof. Adam Wierman, Prof. Eric Mazumdar, and Prof. Steven Low.

Previously, I was a PhD Student at the Automatic Control Laboratory at ETH Zürich under the supervision of Prof. Florian Dörfler (main advisor) and Prof. Alessio Figalli (second advisor). I was part of NCCR Automation. I received my BSc. and MSc. from ETH Zürich in 2016 and 2019, respectively. During my studies, I visited the Massachussets Institute of Technology and wrote my Master's thesis at Stanford University, in Prof. Marco Pavone's Autonomous Systems Lab. In summer 2024, I was an intern in quantitative research at Citadel GQS.

My current research interests include decision-making under uncertainty, game theory, and optimal transport, with applications in autonomy, energy, and biology.
